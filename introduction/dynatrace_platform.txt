dynatrace platform
Dynatrace is a comprehensive software intelligence platform designed to provide end-to-end visibility and insights into
the performance of applications, infrastructure, and user experiences. It leverages advanced artificial intelligence to automate monitoring, detect anomalies, and deliver actionable insights for optimizing digital performance.
The Dynatrace platform is built to support a wide range of technologies and environments, including cloud-native applications, microservices architectures, and traditional on-premises systems. It offers a unified solution that integrates various monitoring capabilities, such as application performance monitoring (APM), infrastructure monitoring, user experience monitoring, log management, and more.
Key features of the Dynatrace platform include:
- **Application Performance Monitoring (APM)**: Provides deep insights into application performance, transaction tracing, and code-level diagnostics to identify bottlenecks and optimize application performance.
- **Infrastructure Monitoring**: Monitors the health and performance of servers, containers, and cloud environments, providing visibility into resource utilization and system health.
- **User Experience Monitoring**: Captures real user interactions and synthetic transactions to assess user experience
